dasha, so are you a yoga teacher or a manager?+
a yoga teacher, an experience architect, an entrepreneur, a producer, a manager. for 15 years i built projects around wellbeing and sport, then turned to the craft itself and started teaching yoga under my own brand. at some point i realized i don't want to separate the roles: i create experience. sometimes it looks like a yoga class — an immersive practice with live music in an old theater. sometimes like a corporate program or a retreat. the form changes, the essence stays.
what is soft power?+
my motto, my philosophy and the name of my brand. soft power is when you change people not with pressure but with a space where it's safe for them to change on their own.
what does "experience architect" mean?+
it's someone who creates a whole experience for a product or an event — online or offline — so that this experience truly changes the person who meets it. concept, meaning, team, space, visuals, music, promotion and the person's path inside — all of it is one system.
wow, can i ask you to do this for our product / brand / company?+
yes. a turnkey event or retreat, a corporate wellbeing program, consulting or a strategy session. you can also invite me to talk about your product — we'll find a point of connection if we share the same values.
what are you looking for now?+
international projects at the intersection of wellbeing, longevity, biohacking, ai, body practices and creativity. partnerships with companies and brands for whom people are not a line in a budget. world-class events and spaces. and people i want to build a new world with.
who do you work with?+
with companies that need real contact, not just team building with masterminds. with people who stand firmly on their feet and understand the value of spiritual practice. with studios and founders of wellbeing projects. and with everyone who steps onto the mat.
and where does yoga go then?+
nowhere. yoga has been my daily practice for more than 15 years. i've studied with teachers all over the world, taken part in international summits and tribe gatherings in paris and barcelona, i keep learning and practice every day. yoga is just one of my facets.
they say you even design vacations?+
yes, i don't really mind what to design)) even my parents' vacation i designed as a unique experience — from the first step to the last: the route, the rhythm, the impressions, the surprises. take a look and get inspired. experience architecture is not a job, it's a way of looking at the world.
where are you based?+
i'm based in lisbon and work worldwide: online, hybrid, or i'll fly over — if the project is worth it.
i wasn't going to read all this. how do i reach you?+
the easiest way is telegram: t.me/beloved_dasha. tell me what your idea or task is — we'll get on a call and talk it through.
