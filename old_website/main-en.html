about me
i’m Dasha

my mission is to create a loving, safe, and integrated space where people can come to know themselves more deeply — their needs, desires, and inner rhythms — and build resilience by reconnecting the mind with the body

for over 15 years, i’ve worked in the wellbeing and sports fields across startups and global companies, while continuously developing not only as a manager, but also as a teacher and practitioner. in 2022, i left the brand team at Nike, changed countries, and chose to fully devote myself to teaching yoga — a practice that has been part of my life for more than 15 years

today, i run several projects centered around yoga and embodied practices and work with a global community of over 2,000 practitioners. i continue to study, practice, and learn with teachers around the world, integrating new experiences into my work and sharing them with those who come to practice with me

i’m currently based in Lisbon, working both in person and online with people from around the world
